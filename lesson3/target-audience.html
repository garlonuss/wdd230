Target audience description

This site focuses on outdoor hobbiests and weekend warriors. People who plan on outdoor activities like sports, camping, hiking, and cetera, and who wish to better plan their activities around the accommodations of nature.

Persona 1

  • Name: Jake Baller
  • Age: 21
  • Occupation: Student / amature athlete
  • Hobbies and Interests: All kinds of sports, particularly baseball; bicycling; weight training
  • Outdoor Goals: Jake has a regular pick-up basketball game with his friends every Wednesday and he likes to try to get a good few innings in on the weekends when he can.

Persona 2

  • Name: Henry Slate
  • Age: 43
  • Occupation: Police Detective
  • Hobbies and Interests: Tent camping, outdoor grilling, fishing, woodworking
  • Outdoor Goals: Henry tries to get out to the mountains at least every other month for a long weekend with his family. They often camp out by the reservoir at Smith and Moorehouse where they use many of the available hiking trails and dutch oven their meals.

Scenarios

  1. Family is coming in for the weekend. Will it be good weather for a barbecue at the park?
  2. I've been practicing for the upcoming mud run. How wet and cold should I expect to get?
  3. I just finished assembling a model rocket. Will the skies be clear for a launch tomorrow?
  4. I've got a site reserved to camp this weekend. How warm should I dress?
  5. Tomorrow's the 4th of July. Will there bee too much wind to safely set of our fireworks?
